How to Play

Select a word by clicking on a space in the grid. Click again to toggle between vertical and horizontal words. Guess a word and find out how well each letter fits.

This works just like Wordle!

C
Guess is correct. This square is unveiled in the solution.
A
Guess is a hidden tile in this (vertical or horizontal) word.
T
Guess isn't in any word containing this cell.

The board to the right shows previous guesses & their results.
